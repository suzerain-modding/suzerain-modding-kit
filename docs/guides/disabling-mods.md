# Disabling Mods

If you want to play without mods, but without uninstalling all your mods. Follow the instructions below. If you want to uninstall mods or uninstall MelonLoader entirely, see [Uninstalling Mods](uninstalling-mods.md).

1. Launch Steam.
2. Select Suzerain in your library.
3. Select the gear icon > Properties.
4. Add `--no-mods` under launch options.
5. Close the preferences window.
6. Launch the game. No mods will be loaded.
7. To enable mods again, remove `--no-mods` from the launch options.
